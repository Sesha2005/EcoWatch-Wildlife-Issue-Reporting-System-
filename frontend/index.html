Protecting Life, One Report at a Time

EcoWatch is a digital bridge between concerned citizens and wildlife authorities. We believe that technology can be a powerful ally in wildlife conservation, providing the tools needed for rapid response and data-driven decision making.

Whether it's poaching activity, habitat destruction, or wildlife-human conflict, EcoWatch ensures that every grievance reaches the right hands instantly.
